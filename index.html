How We Met

It was a rainy Tuesday morning at the little coffee shop on Maple Street. Emma was rushing to get her usual latte before work, and James was sitting by the window, reading his favorite book. When Emma accidentally bumped into his table, spilling coffee everywhere, neither of them knew that this clumsy moment would be the beginning of their greatest adventure.

First Date

Three weeks later, James worked up the courage to ask Emma out. Their first date was a picnic in Central Park, where they talked for hours about dreams, travel, and their shared love of old movies. As the sun set, they both knew this was something special ‚Äì something worth fighting for.

The Proposal

On their fourth anniversary, James recreated their first date in the same spot in Central Park. As Emma was reminiscing about that magical evening, James got down on one knee with a ring hidden in a picnic basket. Through happy tears, she said yes to forever.
